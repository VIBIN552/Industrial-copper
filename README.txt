Industrial Copper Modeling
Overview
This project focuses on modeling the industrial production and application of copper. It includes data analysis, simulation models, and predictive analytics to optimize copper manufacturing processes, cost estimation, and market trends forecasting.

Features
Data Analysis: Analyze historical and real-time data on copper production and market prices.
Simulation Models: Model various industrial processes related to copper, such as smelting, refining, and fabrication.
Predictive Analytics: Forecast future trends in copper demand, supply, and pricing.
Optimization: Identify optimal production strategies to minimize costs and maximize output.
